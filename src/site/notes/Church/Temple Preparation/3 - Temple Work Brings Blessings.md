### Today

> The inspired erection and proper use of temples is one of the great evidences of the divinity of the Lord’s work. … Where there are temples, with the spirit of revelation resting upon those who administer therein, there the Lord’s people will be found; where these are not, the Church and kingdom and the truth of heaven are not.<sup><a href="https://ia600406.us.archive.org/12/items/MormonDoctrine1966/MormonDoctrine1966.pdf#page=550">1</a></sup>

What is actually "Temple Work"
- In very over simplified terms: Performing ordinances
- Ordinances for the Dead Only
	- Baptims
	- Confirmations
- Ordinances for the Living and the Dead
	- Initiatories
	- Endowments
	- Sealings
## Temple Blessings

Temple work—in this dispensation—was reinstated with the Kirtland Temple. Our understanding of the temple; its purpose and function, has pulled into focus the importance of *ordinances and covenants*.

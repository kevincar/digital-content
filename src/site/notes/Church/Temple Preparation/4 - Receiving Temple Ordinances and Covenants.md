# Receiving Temple Ordinances & Covenants

## Covenants

A covenant is a sacred agreement between God and a person or group of people. 
- God sets specific conditions.
- He promises to bless us as we obey those conditions.
- The saving ordinances of the priesthood are always accompanied by covenants.

### Notable Historical/Scriptural Contexts
- The first instance in the bible of a covenant was with Noah:
	- First to Noah as [a promise to save](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/6?lang=eng&id=p18#p18)
	- Second to Noah as [a promise to never flood the earth again](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/9?lang=eng&id=p9-p11#p9)

	- [Land of Inheritance](https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/gen/15?lang=eng&id=p7-p10#p7) - Animal Sacrifice

## Temple Ordinances and covenants

Promises made in the ordinance of the Endowement
> Those who go to the temple have the privilege of taking upon themselves specific covenants and obligations relative to their exaltation and that of others. Elder James E. Talmage wrote:
> > The ordinances of the endowment embody certain obligations on the part of the individual, such as covenant and promise to observe the law of strict virtue and chastity, to be charitable, benevolent, tolerant and pure; to devote both talent and material means to the spread of truth and the uplifting of the race; to maintain devotion to the cause of truth; and to seek in every way to contribute to the great preparation that the earth may be made ready to receive her King,﻿—the Lord Jesus Christ. With the taking of each covenant and the assuming of each obligation a promised blessing is pronounced, contingent upon the faithful observance of the conditions. (⁠The House of the Lord, page 100.)
> We covenant with the Lord to devote our time, talents, and means to His kingdom.
> We are a covenant people. We covenant to give of our resources in time and money and talent﻿—all we are and all we possess﻿—to the interest of the kingdom of God upon the earth. In simple terms, we covenant to do good. We are a covenant people, and the temple is the center of our covenants. It is the source of the covenant.<sup><a href="https://www.churchofjesuschrist.org/study/manual/preparing-to-enter-the-holy-temple/preparing-to-enter-the-holy-temple?lang=eng&id=p166,p167,p168-p169#p166">1</a></sup>
